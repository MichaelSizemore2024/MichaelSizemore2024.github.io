My name is Michael Sizemore, and I have been enrolled as a full-time student majoring in Computer Science for the past three and a half years at the University of Utah. As a full-time student majoring in Computer Science at the University of Utah and graduating this May, I have developed technical skills through creating software applications and working with various languages, as well as teamwork skills through working on team projects with my peers. My experiences have enabled me to develop quality software using good coding practices while maintaining a strong and collaborative connection with my peers.
